Why Choose Us

Make your event truly special and stress-free.

Deevana Art is an event planning company that provides floristry elements into all events, gatherings, etc. We are dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for every occasion, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations. Our team of experienced professionals are passionate about turning your vision into reality, handling every detail with care and creativity. With meticulous planning and innovative ideas, we strive to exceed your expectations and deliver flawless events that leave a lasting impression. Whether it's a corporate event, wedding, or special celebration, we are here to make your event truly special and stress-free.

Based in New York City, NY as of April 2013, Deevana Art has worked its way up to ensuring every event remains a remarkable memory in all of our client’s hearts. The name Deevana Art stands for Crazy Art in Turkmen, the home language of our company. Our founder, Olga Aleksandrova, comes from a city called Ashgabat from Turkmenistan. Her previous experiences within the event industry and personal craftsmanship evolved to her own business within the industry. Her journey towards working on events inspired her to begin her own company and share her own work/dedication to the job.
